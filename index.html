Hiking Trails

Tucson is home to many beautiful hiking trails. Let Warren show you the wonders of the Sonoran Desert on a hike tailored to your skill level. Whether you're looking for a short trip or a longer adventure, she has got you covered. If you're unsure about the right adventure for you, hit Warren up today for a free consultation.

Some popular trails she guides are:

  • Seven Falls (3-5 hours)
  • Mt. Lemmon (4-8 hours)
  • Safford Peak (4-6 hours)
  • Finger Rock (5 hours)
  • Pima Canyon (5 hours)
  • Linda Vista (1-7 hours)
  • And many more

Climbing

Tucson is surrounded by beautiful climbing areas in one of the most accessible and wild environments on the planet. Warren first learned to climb outside in Tucson, and it's a privilege and honor for her to share this rare perspective with you.

The Catalina Highway offers year-round climbing opportunities to satisfy even the boldest adventurer's tastes. This experience can be tailored for absolute beginners or seasoned climbers alike.

Some popular climbing spots she guides are:

  • Windy Point TR Wall (5-10 minute approach)
  • Munchkin Land (30-45 minute approach)
  • TMNT Wall (10-15 minute approach)

Biking Routes

Tucson is a great place for mountain biking, with everything from basic riding on green trails to fast and technical blue and black trails. Warren can discuss your experience and what you want to get out of your vacation to make this the perfect ride for you.

Some popular routes she guides are:

  • 50 Year Trail (green/blue/black)
  • Star Pass (blue)
  • Molino Basin (blue)
  • Tortolita's (green)
