Hiking Trails

Tucson is home to many beautiful hiking trails. Let your guide, Warren, show you the wonders of the Sonoran desert on a hike tailored to your skill level. Whether you're looking for a short trip or a longer adventure, Warren has got you covered.

If you are unsure on the adventure that is right for you I would love to talk about it with you

Some popular trails I guide are:

  • Seven Falls (3-5 hours)
  • Mt. Lemmon (4-8 hours)
  • Safford Peak (4-6 hours)
  • Finger Rock (5 hours)
  • Pima Canyon (5 hours)
  • Linda Vista (1-7 hours)
  • And many more

Climbing

Tucson is surrounded by beautiful climbing in one of the most accessible yet wild enviornments on the planet.

Tucson is where I first learned to climb outside and I would love to share the rare perspective only a climber can gain with you

The catalina highway offers climbing year around that will satifisfy even the bouldest adventurers taste.

This experience can be tailored for the absolute beginner or the seasoned leader.

Some popular climbing spots I guide are:

  • Windy Point TR wall (5-10 minute approach)
  • Munchkin land (30-45 minute approach)
  • TMNT wall(10-15 minute approach)
